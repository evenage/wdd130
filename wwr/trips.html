Trip Descriptions

Peru Rafting

We pick you up at your hotel with our private transportation at 08:45 am. after one and a half hours drive in our private transport into a stunning view of lakes, villages, and towns that we can see between Urcos and Cusipata.we arrive to the put -in at the shores of “Cusipata River Lodge” our private campground, you have time to change our clothes, you will find a wetsuit that will fit you. Afterward, a life-jacket and a paddle, you have to pick up a helmet, once you are ready, our professional Rafting Guides will give you a safety talk and provide you with all the proper equipment for Rafting. This river section is known as Chuquicahuana and the most popular and the most exciting part of the Urubamba River it is located at 1hour 40 minutes southern from Cusco. This river is classified as class II, III and III+. This section has continuous rapids and for one day trip, it’s exciting moments.
